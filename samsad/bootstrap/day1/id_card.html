Resume Proﬁle Guidelines

General guidelines to keep in mind: Keep your proﬁle short. Two to ﬁve phrases written in a bulleted form or brief paragraph will do. Think of the summary as a snapshot of your skills, accomplishments, and knowledge. Label your proﬁle professionally: Summary of Qualiﬁcations, Career Proﬁle, Career Highlights, Professional Summary, or just Summary or Proﬁle. Place your proﬁle section at the top of your resume page, above your work history, so that the employer can see it when they ﬁrst review your resume.
