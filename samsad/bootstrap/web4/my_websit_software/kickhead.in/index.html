our story

We are a group of 10+ "Greenstone" spread out in Noida and Chennai. Having worked at companies like Satvat, McAfee, Intel Security, McGraw Hill Financial & Monotype, we felt that it was time to do something big. In 2014, a bold step was made - breaking out of the comfort zone and making an impact.

With degrees from GREENSTONE CHENNAI & BITS Pilani acting as catalysts, we kept shelling out products for the student community like GovtJob.org, TestPot.com, LogicGuns.com, Placementyogi.com with a simple vision to empower everyone through cutting edge tech.

4 years since this journey started, we have met some great clients on the way - GREENSTONE CHENNAI, Vajya Kumar & Ravi, Kiran Publications and more. We have almost 1 million students attached with us across all of our portals.

Change is the only constant, we keep on evolving our knowledge with continuous learning and being updated about trends. However, some things stand unchanged - that is our integrity & values. We are firm believers in a fair and transparent style of working.
