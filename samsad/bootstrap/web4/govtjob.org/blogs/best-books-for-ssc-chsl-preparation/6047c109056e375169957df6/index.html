Best Books for SSC CHSL Preparation
March 9, 2021 3307 Views

SSC CHSL (Combined Higher Secondary Level) Exam is conducted by Staff Selection Commission.

The exam is divided into 4 sections covering topics from English Language, General Intelligence, Quantitative Aptitude & General Awareness.

The level of SSC CHSL exam is easy & can be cracked easily if right books & study material are referred to.

There are 2 important pillars to crack any exam -

First, preparing from the right study material/ books

Second, practicing a good number of mock tests

With plethora of study material available online, aspirants tend to get confused and this is where the best standard books play their role. Hence, we are listing here the best relevant books (with links to purchase them) a candidate must refer to for SSC CHSL exam preparation:

English Language:

1. Objective General English by S.P. Bakshi - Get it on Amazon

2. Word Power Made Easy by Norman Lewis - Get it on Amazon

3. Quick Learning Objective General English by RS Aggarwal & Vikas Aggarwal - Get it on Amazon

 

General Intelligence:

1. Test of Reasoning by Edgar Thorpe - Get it on Amazon

2. A Modern Approach to Verbal and Non-Verbal Reasoning by RS Aggarwal - Get it on Amazon

3. Analytical Reasoning by MK Pandey - Get it on Amazon

 

Quantitative Aptitude:

1. Quantitative Aptitude by RS Aggarwal - Get it on Amazon

2. Advance Maths by Rakesh Yadav - Get it on Amazon

3. Fast Track by Rajesh Verma - Get it on Amazon

 

General Awareness:

1. Manorama Yearbook 2021 - Get it on Amazon

2. Lucent's General Knowledge - Get it on Amazon

 

These standard books will help aspirants get clarity of concepts and build a strong base to work upon. Once, you are thorough with the concepts, it is imperative to put those concepts into action & practice a lot to score well & get the job.

For better practice & preparation, visit the SSC CHSL Test Series page to get latest pattern, bilingual mock tests plus previous year papers.
