SBI PO Prelims 2020 - Call Letter Released
Dec. 23, 2020 1544 Views

The admit card for SBI PO 2020 Preliminary exam has been released on 22nd December to recruit 2000 vacancies of Probationary Officers through SBI PO 2020-21 Recruitment exam.

Download your SBI Prelims 2020 Call Letter from the Official Website: SBI PO Prelims Admit Card

Enter your registration number and date of birth to login to your account and download the admit card.

SBI PO Prelims 2020 will begin from December 31, 2020 and would conclude on January 5, 2021.
