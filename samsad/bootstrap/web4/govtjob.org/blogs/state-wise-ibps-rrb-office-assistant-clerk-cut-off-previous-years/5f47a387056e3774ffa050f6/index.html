State-Wise IBPS RRB Office Assistant (Clerk) Cut Off [Previous Years]
Aug. 27, 2020 1954 Views

As an aspirant it is important to know the previous year cut-offs of the exam you are planning to appear in. This helps in evaluating the level of competition in the exam & accordingly build a preparation strategy. Hence, here we are providing the state-wise previous year cut-off marks for IBPS RRB Office Assistant (Prelims & Mains).

There are certain factors based on which the IBPS RRB cut-off is declared. This includes:

  • The number of Applicants
  • Difficulty Level of the Exam
  • Previous Year IBPS RRB Cut Off
  • Number of Vacancies

Candidates who attain the minimum qualifying marks in the prelims phase of the examination are called for the mains. The final selection of candidates is based on the marks scored in the Mains examination.
