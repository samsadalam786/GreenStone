List of Important Days in September
Sept. 4, 2020 2223 Views

Important Days & Dates are crucial from the point of view of various exams. They comprise a good enough portion of the the GK section in exams like SSC, Railways and other Government Exams. These can be tough to remember but easy to fetch marks and contribute significantly towards a good score.

So, to aid you enhance your GK and improve your marks, we have compiled this Article that mentions all the important Days & Dates of September. Read carefully as you are sure to see some of these in your upcoming SSC & Banking exams.

Important Days of September:

 

S.No. Date Day
1. 2nd September Coconut Day
2.  5th September Teacher's Day (Dr. Radhakrishnan’s birthday)
3. 8th September International Literacy Day
4. 14th September Hindi Diwas; World First Air Day
5. 15th September Engineer's Day; International Day of Democracy
6. 16th September World Ozone Day
7. 21st September Alzheimer’s Day; International Day of Peace (UN)
8. 22nd September Rose Day (Welfare of cancer patients)
9. 23rd September International Day of Sign Languages
10. 26th September Day of the Deaf; World Contraception Day
11. 27th September World Tourism Day; World Maritime Day
12. 29th September World Heart Day
13. 30th September International Translation Day

 

This completes the 'Important Days of September' article. Read them, revise them & take GS & CA mock tests to practice them.
